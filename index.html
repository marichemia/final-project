About Us

Our zoo was founded in 2012. Our main objective is to protect and care for animals.

How it works

Every animal under our care at Zootopia is a rescue. After they are ready to go back to wilderness and their habitat we make sure to help them get back out there and live their best life! At the moment we have over 200 animals and 47 species.
